\begin{abstract}
We present \raphael, a clinical copilot that integrates retrieval over curated
guidelines, large-language-model reasoning, and deterministic safety guardrails
for decision support. The system supports voice, text, and image inputs, provides
source-linked justifications, and abstains under uncertainty. We report pilot latency,
factuality, and safety outcomes on synthetic and public benchmarks.
\end{abstract}
