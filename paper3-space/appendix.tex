\appendix
\section*{Appendix: Space-Medicine Simulation Details}
\label{sec:appendix}

\subsection{Synthetic Environment}
Generated 1,200 hours of multistream astronaut biometric data (ECG, SpO₂, accelerometer, audio).

\subsection{Domain Adaptation}
Fine-tuning used transfer learning from terrestrial clinical data with 20 \% domain-randomized augmentation.

\subsection{Compute Setup}
Simulation executed on NASA HECC nodes (A100 GPU x 4) with total runtime ≈ 22 h.
