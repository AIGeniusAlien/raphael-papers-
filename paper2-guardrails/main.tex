\begin{abstract}
We describe a layered safety stack for clinical LLMs: retrieval grounding, cost/interaction constraints, uncertainty-aware abstention, deterministic rules, and guideline-filtered RAG. In pilot evaluations we observe improved risk-sensitivity and stable latency. 
\end{abstract}
