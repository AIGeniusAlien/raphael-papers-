\begin{abstract}
We describe a layered safety stack for clinical LLMs: retrieval grounding,
dose/interaction constraints, uncertainty calibration, and abstention. We provide
ablations and reliability curves showing improved risk-sensitivity and stable latency.
\end{abstract}
